What's in it

Four things. That's the whole app.

Live walk stats

Time, steps, and distance visible while you walk. No toggling, no navigating menus mid-walk to find the number you want.

Walk history mosaic

Every day you walked shows up as a dot on a grid. After a few weeks it becomes something worth looking at — and you can share it as an image.

Streak

How many days in a row you've gone out. Simple counter, no gamification layer on top of it.

All on-device

No account, no server. Walk history and preferences stay on your phone. Delete the app and they're gone with it.

FAQ

A few quick answers.

Why location access?

Used during a walk to estimate distance. The app stores the total distance for the session — not a route map or GPS trace.

Why might steps or distance not show?

Step count depends on device motion support. Distance depends on location permission being enabled while the walk is active.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Everything is stored on-device. No internet required to walk, save, or review your history.

Questions or bugs?
