FAQ

Common questions

The app is simple, so the questions are too.

Does it need an account?

No. There is no sign-in flow, no user profile, and no server-side walk history required to use the app.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Walk history and preferences are stored on-device, so the core experience does not depend on a continuous connection.

Why might steps or distance be unavailable?

That usually comes down to device support or permissions. Step count depends on motion data support, and distance depends on location services being enabled.

Can I clear my history?

Yes. Because the data is local to your device, deleting the app removes the locally stored walk history.
