Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app official?

No. WAPS Study 2026 is an unofficial study tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sanctioned by the United States Air Force. Always verify critical information against official Air Force guidance.

What content is the app based on?

The current in-app content version is based on AFH 1 (November 2024), with section summaries, flashcards, drills, and SJT scenarios built around that study scope.

What ranks does it support?

The current study experience is built for Airmen testing for E5 and E6.

How do I reset all progress?

Open the app, go to Settings, and use Reset all progress. That clears stored scores, session history, and section progress from the device.

Does the app require an account or internet connection?

No account is required. Core study content and progress tracking are stored locally on-device so the app is designed to be usable without sign-in or a constant network connection.

I found a content issue or typo. What should I do?

Email ray@dahvio.com with the section code, question ID if available, and what looks wrong. That makes it much faster to fix accurately.
