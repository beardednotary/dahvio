Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app official?

No. Army AFT Calculator is an unofficial reference tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sanctioned by the United States Army. All scores should be verified against official FM 7-22 guidelines.

Which scoring standards does the app use?

The app uses the current FM 7-22 scoring tables for the Army Fitness Test (AFT). Scoring tables are updated with each app release to reflect the latest Army field manual.

My score looks wrong. What should I check?

Verify your age, gender, and that you've entered the correct value for each event: 3 Repetition Maximum Deadlift (lbs), Hand Release Push-Ups (reps), Sprint-Drag-Carry (time), Plank (time), and 2-Mile Run (time). If you still believe there's an error, please contact us.

How does body composition (ABCP) affect my score?

Soldiers must meet Army Body Composition Program (ABCP) standards. If you exceed the screening weight for your height, a body fat tape test is required. Failing ABCP results in an administrative AFT failure regardless of your numeric score.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Army AFT Calculator is fully offline. All scoring logic runs locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted.

Will my inputs be saved if I close the app?

Yes. Your last entered values are automatically saved and restored the next time you open the app. No account required.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a refund request directly through the App Store.
