The status system

Your hydration, tracked like a case file.

Every percentage of your daily goal maps to a status. It climbs as you drink and recedes as the day goes on. The skull is watching either way.

FAQ

A few quick answers.

Do I need an account?

No. The app works without sign-in, registration, or a cloud profile. Your log stays on your device.

Why does it want Health access?

Only if you enable Health sync (a Preserved feature). It writes your logged water to Apple Health — it does not read your existing Health data.

What does Preserved unlock?

Health sync, full streak history, extra status insights, dark mode, the full notification archive, alternate app icons, and widgets — via a single lifetime purchase.
