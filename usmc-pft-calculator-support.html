Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app official?

No. USMC PFT Calculator is an unofficial reference tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sanctioned by the United States Marine Corps. All scores should be verified against official MCO 6100.13A guidelines.

Which scoring standards does the app use?

The app uses the current MCO 6100.13A scoring tables for the Marine Corps Physical Fitness Test (PFT). Scoring tables are updated with each app release to reflect the latest Marine Corps Order.

My score looks wrong. What should I check?

Verify your age, gender, and the correct event type (e.g. pull-ups vs. push-ups). If you are in a Combat Arms MOS (0300, 0800, 1800 series), make sure the Combat Arms toggle is enabled — a higher minimum passing score of 210 applies. If you still believe there's an error, please contact us.

What is the Combat Arms MOS toggle?

Marines in Combat Arms MOSs (Infantry, Artillery, Armor, and related series) are held to a higher minimum passing score of 210 points instead of the standard 150. Enabling this toggle applies that elevated standard to your score classification.

How does body composition (BCA) affect my score?

Marines must pass the Body Composition Assessment before a composite PFT score is valid. The app checks height/weight standards first. If you exceed the weight limit for your height, a tape test body fat percentage is required. Failing BCA results in an administrative PFT failure regardless of your numeric score.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. USMC PFT Calculator is fully offline. All scoring logic runs locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted.

Will my inputs be saved if I close the app?

Yes. Your last entered values are automatically saved and restored the next time you open the app. No account required.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to submit a refund request directly through the App Store.
