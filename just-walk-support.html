Frequently Asked Questions

Does Just Walk require an account?

No. The app is designed to work without sign-in, registration, or a cloud profile.

Why does the app ask for location access?

Location access is used during an active walk to estimate distance traveled. The app stores total distance for the session, not a route map.

Why are steps or distance unavailable?

Step count depends on your device's motion and pedometer support. Distance depends on location services and permission being available while the walk is active.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Just Walk stores walk history and preferences locally on-device, so the core experience does not depend on an account or constant internet connection.

Can I share my walking history?

Yes. The app can generate a share image for the days-you-walked mosaic so you can export that visual using the standard iPhone share sheet.

How do I remove my saved data?

Because walk history is stored locally on your device, deleting the app removes the stored data associated with it.

Where should I report a bug or tracking issue?

Email ray@dahvio.com with what happened, what device you are using, and what you expected to see. That makes it much easier to debug quickly.
